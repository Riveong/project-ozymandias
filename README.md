# 3. Project Information

## Project Title

**AI Brand Ambassador Live Shopping**

## Project Summary

AI Brand Ambassador Live Shopping is an AI-powered platform that enables small businesses and e-commerce sellers to create professional live-shopping promotional content without hiring a human host, influencer, or production team.

Users simply provide product information, a product image, and a purchase link. The system automatically generates a persuasive sales script, creates an AI brand ambassador video using PixVerse, and publishes a shareable landing page with a clear call-to-action.

The entire process transforms a static product listing into a complete live-shopping experience within minutes.

## Target Audience

- Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs/UMKM)
- Shopee sellers
- Tokopedia sellers
- TikTok Shop sellers
- Independent online merchants
- Small brands with limited marketing budgets

## Problem Being Solved

Live shopping has become one of the most effective sales channels in e-commerce. However, most small businesses face significant barriers:

- Hiring hosts, influencers, or brand ambassadors is expensive.
- Producing promotional videos requires scripting, recording, editing, and publishing.
- Many sellers lack the time, skills, or resources to consistently create engaging content.

As a result, smaller businesses struggle to compete with larger brands that have dedicated marketing teams.

Our solution eliminates these barriers by generating an AI-powered virtual brand ambassador that can present products, explain benefits, and drive conversions automatically.

Instead of spending days and significant budgets on content production, sellers can create professional live-shopping content in minutes.

# 4. Project Showcase

## Creative Concept

### "Every Small Business Deserves Its Own Brand Ambassador."

Large brands have marketing teams, influencers, and professional presenters.

Small businesses often have only a product photo and a product description.

AI Brand Ambassador Live Shopping bridges this gap by giving every seller access to an AI-powered salesperson that can work 24/7.

## Workflow

1. Seller enters product information.
2. AI generates a persuasive live-shopping sales script.
3. PixVerse generates a 30+ second AI brand ambassador video.
4. The system automatically creates a landing page containing:
   - Generated video
   - Product summary
   - Product information
   - Buy Now CTA button
5. Seller shares the landing page with customers.

## Innovation

Instead of simply generating a video, the platform delivers a complete conversion funnel:

```text
Product Information
        ↓
AI Sales Script
        ↓
AI Brand Ambassador Video
        ↓
Landing Page
        ↓
Purchase CTA
```

This creates an end-to-end marketing asset rather than a standalone video.

## Impact

The solution democratizes professional marketing content creation for millions of small businesses.

By reducing production cost and time, sellers can launch campaigns faster, increase engagement, and improve sales performance without requiring specialized marketing resources.
